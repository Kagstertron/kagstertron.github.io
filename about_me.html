Esther the Collector | About Me

I started playing Magic at the beginning of 2011 when I finally turned 12.

My brother, 5 years older & a real stickler for the rules, hadn't encouraged me to play before because of the suggested age rating for the game.

However, on my 12th birthday he was the one to buy me my first deck and a metallic blue 20-sided die (that I still have to this day).

Since then, even though my brother has long since moved to another continent, I have become an active member of several MTG groups and even helped set up the Magic the Gathering Society at Bournemouth University.

The first set I ever played was Scars of Mirrodin and many of the cards are still my favourite to this day.
