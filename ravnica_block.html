GUILDS OF RAVNICA

Guilds of Ravnica is set on the plane of Ravnica where everyone is divided into two-colour guilds:

  • The Boros Legion.
  • House Dimir.
  • The Golgari Swarm.
  • The Selesnya Conclave.
  • The Izzet League.
  • The Gruul Clans.
  • The Orzhov Synidate.
  • The Azorius Senate.
  • The Simic Combine.
  • The Cult of Rakdos

This makes multi-coloured cards key in this set.
