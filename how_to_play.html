Esther the Collector | How to Play

Before learning the game, it's worth learning some basic definitions here.

The aim of the game is to get your opponent's life total to 0.

Turn sequencing:

  1. untap
  2. upkeep
  3. draw
  4. main phase
  5. combat
  6. second main phase
  7. endstep

Step 1. To start, shuffle your library and draw 7 cards. You want a mix of lands and lower cost spells in your opening hand. You can mulligan (shuffle and redraw), but you reduce the number of cards you start with each time.

Step 2. Play a land and spells – if you have enough mana!

Step 3. Go to combat! Declare which of your creatures are attacking which opponent.


Step 4. See which creatures your opponent announces as blocking your creatures.


Step 5. Go to damage - see what injuries and fatalities occur from the fighting.

Step 6. In your second mainphase, play any further spells.


Step 7. Declare the end of your turn only once you are completely finished – your opponent might have spells they can cast in your endstep.
